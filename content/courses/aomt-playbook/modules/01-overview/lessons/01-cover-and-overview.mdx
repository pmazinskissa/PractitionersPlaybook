# Welcome to the AOMT Playbook

This playbook is a comprehensive guide for practitioners leveraging AI capabilities to facilitate new ways of working. The **Agentic Operating Model Transformation (AOMT)** provides a non-linear approach to operational transformations that moves farther and faster in achieving an organization's objectives.

<Callout type="concept" title="What You'll Learn">
This course covers the complete AOMT methodology — from diagnosing operational problems with AI-powered data analysis to sustaining improvements through structured governance.
</Callout>

## Course at a Glance

This playbook spans **66 pages** of comprehensive methodology covering:

- **3 Sections:** Practitioners Guide, Sustainment, and Change Management
- **Case Study:** Metro Cable — a worked example illustrating every concept
- **Exercises:** Hands-on practice with AI prompt crafting and analysis techniques
